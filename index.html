AVID transforms schools and districts so that all students will be successful in college, career, and life.

Advancement Via Individual Determination (AVID) operates with one guiding principle: Hold students accountable to the highest standards, provide academic and social support, and they will rise to the challenge. AVID’s kindergarten through higher education system brings research-based curriculum and strategies to students each day that develop critical thinking, literacy, and math skills across all content areas.
